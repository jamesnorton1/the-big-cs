Hi, I'm James.

In 2015 I was diagnosed with stage 3 Hodgkin’s Lymphoma. Before this, I noticed a small lump in my neck which I left untreated for far longer than I like to admit. Though, as an 18 year old, I never once thought it might be cancer.

I’m well now, thankfully, and have since been pursuing a career in digital product design.

I made this website in response to the severe drop in suspected urgent cancer patient referrals during the pandemic. I hope this site reaches someone putting off going to the doctors, or simply reminds others it can happen to anyone; and that it’s really best to nip it in the bud.

For more work from me check out my portfolio.
